    Research

    2019
      Graduate Researcher
      With the support of travel funding, I undertook a three-month research trip to Yale University’s Beinecke & Rare Books Manuscript Library (New Haven, CT) and Vassar College’s Archives & Special Collections (Poughkeepsie, NY) to undertake research on H.D. and Elizabeth Bishop, respectively.
    2018
      Research Assistant
      Research assistant as part of Poetry Matters' Poesis and Therapein season. In this role, I supported the development of a successfull SSHRC Connections Grant: I developed the bibliography; drafted outreach letters and requests for support; organized contributor, collaborator, and participant biographies. I also organized and facilitated six events in the Montreal area, with 10-40 attendees per event.
    2011-2014
      Research Assistant
      As RA to Drs. Demetres Tryphonopoulos and Matte Robinson, I supported the publication of Hirslanden Notebooks: An Annotated Scholarly Edition (ELS Editions, 2015). This included the transcription of hand-written material, proofreading, copyediting, and the research for and preliminary drafting of 500 scholarly annotations.
