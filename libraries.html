Volunteering

Atwater Library and Computer Centre

Circulation Desk

Since August 2024, I have been volunteering weekly at the Atwater Library's Circulation Desk. As the first person people encounter when entering the library, I'm also the person who sets the tone for their visit. By mainintaining a welcoming atmosphere, greeting every visitor with a smile, I hope to make their experience an enjoyable one. I also love chatting about what patrons are reading, and provide them with readers' advisory services in the process.
